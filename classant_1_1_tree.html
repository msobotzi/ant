GetUniquePermutations calculates the unique permutations of the (assumed identical) leaves.

Parameters
leavesthe leaves of the tree for convenience, defines also the order of indices in perms
permspermutations as indices corresponding to vector leaves. Use indices to assign particles to leaves (not leaves to particles)
